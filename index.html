FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure that your designs meet user needs and business goals? +

I begin every project with a deep discovery phase — understanding your audience, your business objectives, and any existing brand guidelines. I combine user research, competitive analysis, and iterative feedback loops to ensure the final design serves both the end user and your business goals.

Do you offer freelance or contract design services? +

Yes! I'm available for freelance, contract, and project-based work. Whether you need a quick design sprint or long-term collaboration, I'm flexible with arrangements. Book a call to discuss your needs.

Can you walk me through a specific project in your portfolio? +

Absolutely. I'd love to walk you through any project that catches your eye. Each project has a story — from the brief and challenges to the design decisions and outcomes. Just reach out and I'll give you a detailed walkthrough.

What tools and software do you use for your design work? +

My primary tools are Figma for UI/UX and prototyping, Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for graphic design work, and Framer for web design and publishing. I'm always learning new tools to stay at the cutting edge.

What is your design process, and how do you approach new projects? +

My process follows: Discover → Define → Design → Deliver. I start with understanding the problem, then move to ideation and wireframing, high-fidelity design, prototyping, and finally handoff with full documentation. Clear communication throughout every stage.
